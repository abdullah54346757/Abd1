Customer Service

We're here to help! Find answers to common questions and reach out to us if you need further assistance.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

1. How can I track my order?

You will receive an email with tracking details once your order has shipped. You can also log into your account to view the order status at any time.

2. What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy on all jewelry items. If you're not completely satisfied with your purchase, you can return it for a full refund or exchange. Items must be returned in their original condition.

3. Do you offer gift wrapping?

Yes! We offer complimentary gift wrapping for all purchases. Simply select the gift wrap option at checkout.

4. How do I contact customer service?

You can reach our customer service team via email at support@jewelrycollection.com or by calling our toll-free number at 1-800-123-4567. We're here to help!

Shipping Policy

We offer free standard shipping on all orders over $100. Orders are typically processed within 1-2 business days, and delivery times vary depending on your location.

  • Standard Shipping: 5-7 business days
  • Express Shipping: 2-3 business days
  • Overnight Shipping: Next business day

Returns and Exchanges

If you are not completely satisfied with your purchase, you can return it within 30 days of receiving your order. Please ensure that the item is in its original condition and packaging.

For returns and exchanges, please follow the instructions included with your order, or contact our customer service team for assistance.
